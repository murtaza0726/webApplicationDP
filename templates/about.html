About Dataset

Healthy Lifestyle Report

Healthy Lifestyle Cities Report for the year 2021 is included in the dataset. It includes some of the world's busiest cities, such as Vancouver, Mumbai, and Amsterdam, and ranks them based on characteristics such as sunshine hours, obesity levels, and life expectancy. Here you'll find information on 1 litre bottle costs as well as happiness hours. There are also statistics on how many hours an average person works and how much time they spend enjoying themselves outdoors.

Visualization

The data is presented in the form of a bar chart, with the measurements of obesity level versus city. Using this graph, we can readily determine which city is the healthiest.
